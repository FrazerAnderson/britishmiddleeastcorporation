About us

The Company

The Management of BMEC, as and together with that of its many Partners and Associates, holds a broad and in-depth level of expertise, with members having extensive senior management experience in such disciplines and industries as Finance, Import/Export, Insurance, Logistics, Marketing, Merchant Services, Trade, Transportation and Tourism.

Our Ethos ...

BMEC is socially responsible and works on a neutral and non-political basis. The ethos and collective value of BMEC is that as an entity it enables the functions and attributes of supply, support businesses and customers in many different countries, to operate or otherwise be consummated on a reliable and commercially competitive basis. These teaming efforts also include the provisioning of creative solutions and structures for specific client needs or transactional requirements that can make the critical difference between a ‘deal’ being completed or lost!
