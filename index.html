British Middle East Corporation (BMEC)

British Middle East Corporation Limited (BMEC), is a private company, registered in England & Wales and based in London (UK). Having both an international presence and representatives throughout the globe, BMEC works extensively but not predominantly with clients throughout the Middle East, Africa and Asia, providing comprehensively within the commodities sector, in various capacities, as Merchants, Agents, Brokers and/or Traders. Informally part of a leading group, BMEC also has several J/V agreements in place to help facilitate its business. Additionally, its infrastructure, supported by that of its ‘group’ capabilities, also assists the various partners and associates with Commodity transactions and related activities, including that of financial, operational and logistical issues.
